Guimaras : Accommodation

Known as the "Mango Capital of the Philippines”, Guimaras is one of the country’s most amazing destinations, sandwiched between Panay and Negros Islands. It was once a sub-province of Iloilo before becoming a Western Visayas province on its own. In this Travel Guide, we've listed the best places for you to explore first—then you can allow Guimaras to uncover the rest of its sublime secrets with each visit.






Guimaras is an island province surrounded by body of water in which the largest is the Guimaras Strait on the western side of the island. It is the most important water body used for navigation. Large and small boats going in and out of the provinces of Iloilo and Guimaras pass Guimaras Strait which makes it an important economic driver that facilitates economic activities for both provinces.

  1. Czech Beach House Adults Only

  2. Nature's Eye Resort

    3. Situated in Guimaras, Nature's Eye Resort offers beachfront accommodation 2.4 km from Guisi Beach and features various facilities, such as a restaurant, a garden and a private beach area.



  3. SunSea Resort

    4. SunSea Resort has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Guimaras. With a shared lounge, the property also features a garden, as well as a private beach area. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, room service and organising tours for guests.



  4. The Clara Beach

    5. The Clara Beach features a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and garden in Guimaras. With free WiFi, this 2-star resort offers room service and a concierge service. Free private parking is available and the resort also provides car hire for guests who want to explore the surrounding area.

  7. Raymen Beach Resort

    8. Welcome to Raymen Beach Resort, your Guimaras Island “home away from home.” Raymen Beach Resort aims to make your visit as relaxing and enjoyable as possible, which is why so many guests continue to come back year after year.



  8. Jannah Glycel Beach House

Superb facilities and an excellent location make the Jannah Glycel Beach House the perfect base from which to enjoy your stay in Guimaras Island.
